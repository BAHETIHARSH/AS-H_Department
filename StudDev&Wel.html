YES!+ COURSE

YOUTH EMPOWERMENT and SKILL DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMME


Objective : To develop 1. Mental clarity 2. Emotional strength 3. Stress Management 4. Confidence 5. Leadership Quality 6. Sense of Responsibility 7. Overall Personality Development
Yes!+ Activities : YES!+ course is of 25 hours programme, taught over 6 consecutive days for 3-4 hours per day. Since academic year 2012-13, it is organized at first year level. Workshop Modules comprise of : 1. Group discussions 2. Role-play 3. Team games 4. Talks and presentations 5. Breathing techniques 6. Asanas and Pranayama



GUEST LECTURES


Objective : 1. To facilitate interaction with experts of various areas 2. To create awareness about the recent trends 3. Overall Personality Development
Activities : At PCCOE, emphasis is given not only making students academically strong, but true leaders and team players, thus preparing them for the real life world. FE organizes expert lectures on different topics like: 1. Time management 2. Goal Setting 3. Success programming 4. Stress management 5.Life management skills 6. Patent Laws 7. Expert Session for difficult topics, etc.



READERS CLUB


Objective : 1. To provide books to improve the reading habit 2. To develop awareness in students about importance of books and other resources as a means of recording and sharing human achievements, failures and aspirations 3. To provide help for enhancing the knowledge of students
Facilities : : 1. Free access of books on communication skill, motivational and spiritual and biographies 2. Knowledge sharing sessions

CHEMISTRY CLUB


Objective : To give hands on training to understand 1. Water purification techniques 2. Basic concepts of electrochemistry 3. Separation and analysis of mixtures of compounds

Industrial Visit

Chemistry Club of First Year Department has organized Industrial Visit to Water treatment Plant, Nigdi, Pune. on 20/10/2018. The objective of this visit was to expose the students to various steps involved in purification of water done by Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation. The topic “Water treatment Plant” is part of syllabus of Engineering Chemistry under SPPU. Total 48 students participated in this visit.



MATHOLIC


Objective : To improve the logical and mathematical ability of the students
Activity : Solutions to logical and mathematical puzzles In this activity we display one puzzle every week, on the basis of fast and correct submission, we identify winners



“Matlab and Multisim” Workshop

Technoscience club of FE has organized one-day workshop on introduction of Multisim and Matlab on 17/11/2018.The Objective of the workshop is to introduce Multisim and Matlab to First Year Engg. Students in order to enhance their knowledge and create awareness in the field of circuit simulation. Through this workshop students can simulate different Electronics and Electrical circuits from their syllabus, which helps their better understanding of concepts. Total 53 students attended this workshop.
