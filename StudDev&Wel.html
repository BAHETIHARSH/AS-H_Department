YES!+ COURSE

YOUTH EMPOWERMENT and SKILL DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMME


Objevtive : To develop 1. Mental clarity 2. Emotional strength 3. Stress Management 4. Confidence 5. Leadership Quality 6. Sense of Responsibility 7. Overall Personality Development
Yes!+ Activities : YES!+ course is of 25 hours programme, taught over 6 consecutive days for 3-4 hours per day. Since academic year 2012-13, it is organized at first year level. Workshop Modules comprise of : 1. Group discussions 2. Role-play 3. Team games 4. Talks and presentations 5. Breathing techniques 6. Asanas and Pranayama



GUEST LECTURES


Objevtive : : 1. To facilitate interaction with experts of various areas 2. To create awareness about the recent trends 3. Overall Personality Development
Activities : At PCCOE, emphasis is given not only making students academically strong, but true leaders and team players, thus preparing them for the real life world. FE organizes expert lectures on different topics like: 1. Time management 2. Goal Setting 3. Success programming 4. Stress management 5.Life management skills 6. Patent Laws 7. Expert Session for difficult topics, etc.



RESDERS CLUB


Objevtive : : 1. To provide books to improve the reading habit 2. To develop awareness in students about importance of books and other resources as a means of recording and sharing human achievements, failures and aspirations 3. To provide help for enhancing the knowledge of students
Facilities : : 1. Free access of books on communication skill, motivational and spiritual and biographies 2. Knowledge sharing sessions
Activities : 1. Logo and Motto making competition 2. Poster making competition on various topics like: Why Reading Habit is Important, Clean PCCoE, Fly High with Reading etc., Elocution Competition: Technology Makes Or Shakes



WAL MAGAZINE


Objevtive : : 1. To identify and nurture the creativity among students 2. To provide opportunities to express their views 3. To provide help for enhancing the knowledge of students
Activities : 1. Monthly displays in form of poems, articles, stories, photos, paintings, sketches , etc 2. Word Study: to improve the vocabulary
Competitions : : 1. Photography competition on CAMPUS THROUGH YOUR LENS 2. An activity on the topic “A Unique way of presenting social issue” in form of : articles, drawings, PowerPoint presentations, posters and poems
