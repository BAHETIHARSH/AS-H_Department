Spectrum

State level Annual Tech Fest for First Year Students

Spectrum

In Ac. Yr. 2018-19; First Year Department successfully organized "SPECTRUM" an annual technical symposium of FE. This symposium was initiated in 2008 and successively 12 years we conducted it with a grand success. Due to pandemic in 2019-20 it was not conducted but in 2020-21 we conducted this event in online platform with huge number of participants.The main objective to provide a platform exclusively for First Year engineering students to express and apply their innovative ideas and hidden talents in the competitive technological world and to develop a global perspective as well as leadership skills among FE students.

  • It is a Science and Technology festival organised by PCCoE.
  • It is specially for First year engineering students.
  • Provides platform to students to develop and showcase their technical skills.
